Who we are: MyGrind is a youth baseball and softball training app operated by My Grind Sports LLC.

1. Introduction

MyGrind ("we," "us," "our," or the "Service") is a web-based training and development platform for youth baseball and softball players, operated by My Grind Sports LLC ("Company") based in Santa Clarita, California. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use mygrindapp.com (the "Service").

By using MyGrind, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Who Can Use MyGrind

MyGrind is designed for use by:

  • Parents and legal guardians who are at least 18 years old, who create and own accounts on behalf of their player(s).
  • Players under 13, who use MyGrind only through a Family-plan profile created and owned by their parent or legal guardian, under the verifiable parental consent process described in Section 6.5.
  • Players ages 13 through 17, who use MyGrind through profiles created and owned by their parent or legal guardian.
  • Players age 18 or older, who may create their own accounts independently.

Children under 13 cannot create their own MyGrind account. A child profile under 13 only exists when a parent on an active Family plan adds it through the parent's account.

6.5 Children Under 13 (COPPA)

MyGrind supports parent-managed accounts for players under 13 on the Family plan. The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA") sets out specific rules for any service that collects personal information from a child under 13. This section explains exactly how those rules apply to MyGrind.

6.5.1 Information We Collect from Children Under 13

When a parent adds a child profile under 13 to their MyGrind Family account, we collect from or about that child:

  • First name (or nickname) entered by the parent
  • Age entered by the parent
  • Sport (baseball or softball)
  • Position (optional, entered by the parent)
  • Training journal entries, workout logs, goals, streaks, and stats that the child or parent enters into the child's profile

We do not collect a separate email address, phone number, or login credential for a child under 13. The child uses the parent's account; there is no independent sign-up path for a child.

6.5.2 Verifiable Parental Consent

We use the parent's payment of an active Family plan subscription as verifiable parental consent. The parent enters card details with Stripe at signup, the charge is processed in the parent's name, and only after that charge succeeds does the parent gain access to add child profiles. The Federal Trade Commission recognizes a verified payment transaction as one of the listed methods for obtaining verifiable parental consent under 16 CFR § 312.5. This is the same approach used by Khan Academy Kids, ClassDojo, and Apple Family Sharing.

If the Family subscription lapses, no new child profiles can be added. Existing child data remains under the parent's exclusive control and the parent may still review or delete it at any time.

6.5.3 Parental Rights for Children Under 13

The parent or legal guardian who set up the Family account has, at all times, the right to:

  • Review every field collected about their child by signing in, opening the profile switcher at the top of the journal, selecting the child's profile, and viewing the profile and entries directly
  • Update or correct any field by editing it in the child's profile
  • Delete the child's entire profile (and every entry under it) by selecting the child in the switcher and tapping "Delete this profile" in the switcher bar, or by emailing support@mygrindapp.com
  • Refuse any further collection of the child's personal information by deleting the child's profile and not adding another

We will not condition a child's participation in any activity on the child or parent disclosing more personal information than is reasonably necessary to use the Service.

6.5.4 Data Minimization for Children Under 13

We do not send marketing email or marketing SMS to any child under 13. We do not share, sell, or disclose a child's personal information to any third party for advertising or behavioral targeting. The only third parties that process a child's data are the service providers listed in Section 5.1 (Firebase for storage, and the operational vendors that keep the Service running). No child data is provided to Mailchimp.

A child's profile data is retained only while the parent's account holds the profile. When the parent deletes a child profile, we delete the profile document and every journal entry under it from Firestore.

10. SMS Communications

What we use SMS for: When a parent or account holder enters a player's mobile phone number on Screen 6 of the signup flow and presses "Send Invite," MyGrind sends a one-time SMS to the player containing the player's first name and a link to set up their profile. If the player does not have their own phone, the parent may enter their own mobile number instead, and the one-time invite or verification text is sent to the parent's phone. We may also send transactional notifications to the parent's own phone (for example, when a coach replies to a feedback request). MyGrind does not send marketing SMS.

Phone numbers we collect: Parent's mobile phone (entered during signup), player's mobile phone (entered by the parent on Screen 6), and coach or instructor phone numbers (entered by the player when sending a feedback request).

Consent: Phone numbers are submitted only after the parent or player has reviewed a confirmation screen, checked a standalone consent box agreeing to receive the messages, and explicitly tapped a Send button. We do not import phone numbers from third parties or auto-enroll users in SMS programs.

No sharing for marketing: Mobile opt-in phone numbers are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Frequency and rates: Message frequency varies based on use of the Service. Standard message and data rates may apply per your wireless carrier's plan.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to any MyGrind SMS to unsubscribe from further messages to that number. Reply HELP for support, or email support@mygrindapp.com. Opting out of SMS does not affect non-SMS use of the Service.

Retention: Phone numbers are stored for the duration of the active account. SMS delivery logs are retained by our messaging provider (Twilio) per their standard retention policy. We delete phone numbers from our records within 30 days of account deletion.

Carrier disclosure: Mobile carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
