Questions parents ask

Is MyGrindapp a dating or social app?
No. MyGrindapp is a daily training journal for baseball and softball players, their parents, and coaches. It is not a dating app, hookup app, social network, or messaging service. MyGrindapp.com is operated by My Grind Sports LLC and exists only to help players track practice, games, stats, and progress.
What is MyGrind, exactly?
MyGrind is a daily training journal for serious baseball and softball players, plus the parents and coaches in their corner. Players log games, practices, lifts, arm care, grades, and rest. It is built to develop the whole athlete, not just track stats, drawing on 35 years in the game as a coach, trainer, dad, and mentor.
Is my player's data secure and private?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit with HTTPS, entries are stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, and account credentials are stored encrypted. A player's journal is private to their account. We never sell personal information and never run third-party advertising inside the app.
Who can see what my player writes in the journal?
Parents create and own the account, and the daily journal stays the player's own space. Parents stay connected through a weekly progress digest, emailed every Monday, that summarizes activity, streaks, and standing rather than turning the journal into something written for an audience. Coaches only see the specific notes a player chooses to send them.
Can my player use MyGrind without their own phone or email?
Yes. A parent or adult creates the account with their own email, then adds the player's profile. Your player does not need their own phone, email, or login. They just open MyGrind on the family device and start logging. For players under 13, this parent-managed setup is required.
Do you sell our data or advertise to players?
Never. We do not sell or rent personal information, and there is no third-party advertising inside the app. We never send marketing to a child under 13, and a child's data is never shared for advertising or behavioral targeting.
How much does MyGrind cost, and is the trial really free?
Plans are $9.99 per month or $99.99 per year for one player, or $149.99 per year for a Family plan covering up to 3 players in one household. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial — no card required to start. You only add a card if you decide to keep MyGrind after the trial, so there is no charge and nothing to cancel if it is not a fit.
Do we need to download anything from the App Store?
No. MyGrind runs in any browser at mygrindapp.com and works on iPhone, Android, and desktop. There is no App Store or Google Play download. On a phone you can add it to your home screen for a full app-like experience that opens with one tap.
