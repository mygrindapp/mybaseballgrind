Install MyGrind on your phone

One-tap access from your home screen. Loads instantly. Looks and works like any other app. No App Store needed.

📱 iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open MyGrind in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
🤖 Android (Chrome)
  1. Open MyGrind in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right)
  3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  4. Confirm "Install"

Done in 5 seconds. After that, MyGrind opens straight from your home screen.
